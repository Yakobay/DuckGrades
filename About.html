About DuckGrades

DuckGrades is a website dedicated to calculating your grade, and helping you to succeed academically by suggesting grades for you to aim for, to reach your desired grades.

How to Use DuckGrades

On the homepage, you must name each category, subcategory, and assignment. You can add more categories, subcategories, and assignments by using the buttons on the right side of the page. Use the drop down menus above the buttons to select which category, or subcategory you are putting the new subcategory or assignment in. You can fill out the individual grades for each assignment, or average for each subcategory or category, or leave them blank. You must input the weight of each category, subcategory, and assignment. For each category, the weight is based off of the percent of your overall grade that the category is. For each subcategory, the weight is the percentage of the subcategory's category that the subcategory is. The weight of each assignment should be an integer that the assignment's grade will be mutiplied by when the average of the subcategory is calculated. You must also insert your desired grade on the bottom of the page. When all the grades, names, and weights have been inputted, hit the submit button on the bottom of the grade to calculate your grade!

How DuckGrades Calculates Your Average

Duckgrades calculates your average by adding all the categories together, multiplied by their weight. If a category average is left blank, it is calculated by adding together the averages of the subcategories multiplied by their weights. If a subcategory verage is left blank, the subcategory average is calculated by adding all of the given assignment grades of the subcategory, multipleid by their assignment weights, then dividing them by the amount of assignments.

How DuckGrades Suggests How You Can Get Your Desired Grades

Duckgrades suggests scores for you to achieve by filling in the assignment grades with the minimum grade you would have to achieve in each blank assignment, to achieve the average in each subcategory that would allow you to reach a category average that you will let you achieve your desired grade.
